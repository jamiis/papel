papel - blog feed

vision
*centralize (in-depth) blog reading
*simply add blogs to your feed (blog platform agnostic)
*single page app
*infinite scroll
*minimal, minimal, minimal (e.g. no inbox/new posts. just feed of blog titles)

*twitter, 140 char fire hose of shit
